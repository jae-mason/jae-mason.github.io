Emotional Intelligence

What is emotional intelligence?

EQ (emotional quotient) or emotional intelligence is having the ability to identify, evaluate, control and express emotions. Having a high EQ means you have the ability to understand and connect well with the people around you.

How is it different to IQ?

IQ (intelligence quotient) is a score taken from a standardised test designed to assess your level of intelligence. This is used to define ones academic ability and identify those with high or low IQ.

Why is it important?

It is important because it's a much more useful indicator of how successful you will be in life across various areas (personal and business); this is key to how one migt function in a relational environment. It is in many ways the essence of being human. Our ability to draw on it can assist with looking after our physical, mental and spiritual well-being.
